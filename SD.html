Stories:Dreams

My first exhibition with a podcast

The goal of this project is to creatively express myself and make my voice heard! As part of this project, I participated in drawing sessions, created my own collection of artworks, and recorded an audio story about my life, dreams, and goals. In the end, an exhibition was organized where my works and stories, as well as those of other participants, were presented!
